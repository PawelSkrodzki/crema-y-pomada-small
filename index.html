Design studio

LCrema y Pomada is a design studio with a Barcelona heart and an international soul. Inspired by mother nature’s elegant shapes and genuine essence, our design aims to help purposeful fashion and lifestyle brands find their true visual voice and, thus, turn their ambitions into reality. At Crema y Pomada we put passion in everything we create – from little illustrations to entire websites – because we truly believe in the power of visuals for changing things. For that, just like our muse, our patterns always hold grace and our aesthetics always carry meaning.
